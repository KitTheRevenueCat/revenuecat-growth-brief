Weekly operator brief

What needs attention this week

Deterministic
Priority 1

Acquisition quality check

Trial volume increased, but conversion quality fell. Top-of-funnel growth is outpacing monetization efficiency.

Evidence: Trials 37.3% vs prior period while conversion rate moved -9.5%.

Investigate next: Investigate paywall fit first: review offer mix, price anchoring, and whether channel mix shifted toward lower-intent traffic.

Priority 2

Trial conversion weakened

Trial conversion rate fell 9.5% compared with the prior period.

Evidence: Recent average conversion rate 39.4% vs prior 43.6%.

Investigate next: Inspect paywall message-to-offer fit, introductory offer mix, and whether a traffic/channel shift is bringing in lower-intent users.

Priority 3

Churn pressure increased

Churn moved up 16.9% versus the prior period.

Evidence: Recent average churn signal 4.0% vs prior 3.4%.

Investigate next: Review cancellation timing, renewal-cycle cohorts, and whether a recent acquisition push brought in lower-retention subscribers.

Priority 4

Top-of-funnel grew faster than conversion

Trial starts increased 37.3%, but conversion did not improve alongside them.

Evidence: Trials recent 221 vs prior 161.

Investigate next: Treat this as a traffic-quality or paywall-fit investigation. More trials are only good if conversion and retention stay healthy.
